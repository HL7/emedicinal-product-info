Composition (ePI)

Clinical document used to represent the Electronic Product Information (ePI) data set. An Electronic Product Information (ePI) document is an authorised, statutory product information for medicines (i.e. summary of product characteristics, package leaflet and labelling) in a semi-structured format created using the common EU electronic standard.
