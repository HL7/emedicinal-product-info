1. What WonderDrug is and what it is used for

WonderDrug contains the active substance paracetamol. It is an analgesic and antipyretic medicine.

WonderDrug is used for the symptomatic treatment of mild to moderate pain and/or fever.

2. What you need to know before you take WonderDrug

Do not take WonderDrug if you are allergic to paracetamol or any of the other ingredients.

Warnings and precautions: Talk to your doctor before taking WonderDrug if you have liver or kidney problems.

3. How to take WonderDrug

Always take this medicine exactly as described in this leaflet or as your doctor has told you.

The recommended dose is 1 tablet every 4 to 6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.

4. Possible side effects

Like all medicines, this medicine can cause side effects, although not everybody gets them.

Stop taking the medicine and tell your doctor immediately if you experience allergic reactions such as skin rash or swelling.

5. How to store WonderDrug

Keep this medicine out of the sight and reach of children.

This medicine does not require any special storage conditions.

6. Contents of the pack and other information

What WonderDrug contains: The active substance is paracetamol. Excipients: pregelatinized starch, povidone K30, stearic acid, talc, magnesium stearate, hypromellose.

What WonderDrug looks like: White round tablets available in blister packs of 30 or 50 tablets.
