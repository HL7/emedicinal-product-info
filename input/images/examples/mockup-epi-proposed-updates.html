✎ Fix 1 — Contraindications section header icon alignment

Before / After comparison. The icon must sit in the upper-right, matching Indications and Interactions.

❌ Before (icon floats below text)
⛔ Contraindications 2
⛔
⊘Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh C)
⊘Known hypersensitivity to WonderDrug or any excipient
✔ After (icon upper-right, matching other sections)
⛔ Contraindications 2
⊘Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh C)
⊘Known hypersensitivity to WonderDrug or any excipient
Root cause: The .cscard summary tiles use justify-content:space-between; align-items:flex-start which correctly places the icon in the upper-right corner. The .clsh (section header) had an implicit flex-direction:column applied in some render paths, pushing the count badge below the title.

Fix: Ensure .clsh always uses display:flex; flex-direction:row; align-items:center (already enforced in the stylesheet above). The icon/count badge ordering in the markup is: .clsicon → .clstitle (flex:1) → .clscount — no structural change needed.
