1. Name of the medicinal product

WonderDrug 100 mg, 200 mg and 500 mg tablets (paracetamol)

2. Qualitative and quantitative composition

Each tablet contains 100 mg, 200 mg or 500 mg of paracetamol.

Excipients: Pregelatinized starch, povidone K30, stearic acid, talc, magnesium stearate, hypromellose.

3. Pharmaceutical form

Tablet. White, round tablets (100 mg and 500 mg) and green hexagonal tablets (200 mg).

4. Clinical particulars

Indications: Symptomatic treatment of mild to moderate pain and fever in adults and children over 12 years.

Contraindications: Hypersensitivity to paracetamol or any excipients. Severe hepatic impairment.

Dose: Adults and children over 12 years: 500 mg every 4–6 hours. Maximum 4 g (8 tablets of 500 mg) in 24 hours.

5. Pharmacological properties

Paracetamol is an analgesic and antipyretic. ATC Code: N02BE01.

6. Pharmaceutical particulars

Storage: No special storage conditions required. Keep out of reach of children.

Shelf life: 36 months.
