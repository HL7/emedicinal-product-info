Package Leaflet: Information for the user

1. What WonderDrug is and what it is used for

WonderDrug contains the active substance paracetamol. It is an analgesic and antipyretic medicine.

WonderDrug is used for the symptomatic treatment of mild to moderate pain and/or fever.

2. What you need to know before you take WonderDrug

Do not take WonderDrug:

  • If you are allergic to paracetamol or any of the other ingredients of this medicine.

Warnings and precautions:

Talk to your doctor or pharmacist before taking WonderDrug if you have liver or kidney problems.

3. How to take WonderDrug

Always take this medicine exactly as described in this leaflet or as your doctor or pharmacist has told you. Check with your doctor or pharmacist if you are not sure.

The recommended dose is 1 tablet every 4 to 6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 6 tablets in 24 hours.

4. Possible side effects

Like all medicines, this medicine can cause side effects, although not everybody gets them.

Stop taking the medicine and seek immediate medical attention if you experience allergic reactions such as skin rash or swelling.

5. How to store WonderDrug

Keep this medicine out of the sight and reach of children.

Do not use this medicine after the expiry date which is stated on the carton.

Store in the original package in order to protect from moisture.

6. Contents of the pack and other information

What WonderDrug contains:

  • The active substance is paracetamol. Each tablet contains 500 mg of paracetamol.
  • The other ingredients are carnauba wax, silicon dioxide, croscarmellose sodium, glyceryl dibehenate, hypromellose, polydextrose, polyethylene glycol, corn starch, titanium dioxide, ferrosoferric oxide, propylene glycol, and sodium lauryl sulfate.

What WonderDrug looks like and contents of the pack:

WonderDrug tablets are white, round, and flat. They are available in blister packs containing 30 tablets.
